In the first– and second-year undergraduate courses, I am responsible for lecturing, grading, proctoring exams, and recording final grades.

**Course focus:**  
- Developing academic English proficiency with an emphasis on international business contexts.  
- Strengthening students’ skills in reading, writing, listening, and speaking for academic and professional communication.  

**Responsibilities:**  
- Designed and delivered lectures and in–class activities for multiple sections of first– and second–year students.  
- Developed course materials and assessments aligned with program learning outcomes.  
- Provided feedback on students’ written work and presentations to improve academic English usage.
